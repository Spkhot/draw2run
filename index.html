Getting Started is Simple

Setup a gesture language on your laptop in under 2 minutes.

No Installer Needed

Download the standalone GestureFlow.exe binary. It is a portable app containing PySide6, Python, and the gesture hook modules inside a single executable file.

SmartScreen Warning: Because the binary is self-compiled and unsigned, Windows Defender may show a pop-up on first run. Click "More Info" and then "Run anyway".

Map Actions & Train Shape

Go to the Add Gesture tab inside the application:

  • Input a unique identifier (e.g. "Spotify").
  • Choose an Action Type (like Open App or Media Control) and set the path/value.
  • Draw your custom shape on the training canvas 3 times. Tracing over the light cyan reference marks helps align patterns.

Traces: 3 / 3

Double-Tap and Draw

Minimize the application to your background system tray.

  • Double-tap the Ctrl key on your keyboard, OR double-click the Right Mouse Button (configured in Settings).
  • Your screen dims, indicating that the full-desktop overlay canvas is active.
  • Draw your custom shape anywhere on the screen by dragging the mouse, and release. The action runs immediately!

Run Automatically on Boot

You don't need to manually configure Windows scripts to ensure it runs every time you open your laptop.

  • Go to the **Settings** tab.
  • Check the checkbox **"Launch GestureFlow automatically on Windows startup"**.
  • Click **Save Settings**. The application automatically updates the Windows user registry.
